And so he said:

\Q[
Something very smart

And with multiple paragraphs.
]

and it was great.

And so he said:
Something very smart
And with multiple paragraphs.
and it was great.

And so he said:

> Something very smart

  And with multiple paragraphs.

and it was great.
which renders as:
And so he said:
Something very smart
And with multiple paragraphs.
and it was great.
