The source is automatically inferred for certain known websites, e.g.:
  • Wikimedia https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons
    • from: upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/68/Akha_cropped_hires.JPG
    • to: en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Akha_cropped_hires.JPG
