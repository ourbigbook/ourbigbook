Header (\H)

 Home OurBigBook Markup Macro
Tags: Block macro Words: 6k Articles: 67
Shorthand syntax with = (equal sign followed by a space):
= My h1

== My h2

=== My h3
Shorthand headers end at the first newline found. They cannot therefore contain raw newline tokens.
Equivalent sane:
\H[1][My h1]

\H[2][My h2]

\H[3][My h3]
Custom ID for internal links on shorthand headers:
= My h1
{id=h1}

== My h2
{id=h2}

=== My h3
{id=h3}
Sane equivalent:
\H[1][My h1]{id=h1}

\H[2][My h2]{id=h2}

\H[3][My h3]{id=h3}
